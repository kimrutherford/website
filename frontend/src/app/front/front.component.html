Welcome to the new PomBase website
Although it's still under development, nightly data updates are working and several new features are now available:
  • Publication pages
  • Genotype pages
  • Improved gene pages with reduced redundancy in annotation summary views
  • Improved ontology term pages showing annotated descendant terms and links to ancestor terms
  • Phenotypic "grouping" filters on gene pages and phenotype ontology term pages
Over the next few months we plan to add:
  • Improved query interface
  • JBrowse genome browser
  • Protein feature viewer
  • Network viewer
Please tell us what you think of the new look and function of the PomBase pages, and what you'd most like to see next.
